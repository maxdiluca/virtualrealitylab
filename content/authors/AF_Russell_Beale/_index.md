Professor Russell Beale is Professor of Human-Computer Interaction in the School of Computer Science at the University of Birmingham. His work focuses on how people use, adapt and are affected by computer and communication technologies, with interests spanning usability, internet and mobile systems, human-computer interaction, AI, privacy and security.
