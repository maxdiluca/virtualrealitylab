The VR Lab was pleased to host students and colleagues as part of the University of Birmingham's [BSURE](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/about/college-of-engineering-and-physical-sciences/computer-science/about/bsure) summer research programme in 2025.

BSURE gives undergraduate students an opportunity to experience research through project work, technical development and collaboration with academic teams. The group photo marks the end of a summer of research activity connected to immersive technologies, interaction design and applied XR.
